📘 Your Guide

This site has a number of features to make your life easier and cheaper. You will notice that there are no images, mostly text and emojis. The reason for this is because there are times when we are updating information using hospital WiFi or at 3am. This is where Night mode helps those tired eyes! The aim is to make life easier for you and for those helping you with your child care. Families are made up in so many ways and go through so many situations. This can help when attending a GP, Hospital or with family/friends who help with childcarr.

Quick logging for busy moments. All data stays in your own Google Sheet.

🎯 Mission

Empower parents and caregivers with a simple, reliable tool.

🧭 Tab Guide

  • 🤱 Milk: Pump & feed logging. This will show you when you may be running low, your 7 day moving trend and both left and right output.
  • 🍼 Formula: Formula feeds. This will help track how much your child is drinking and also help with planning how much to buy.
  • 🧻 Nappy: Wet/dirty nappies. A way to track the trend and also to help to know when you may run out!
  • ❤️ Tummy/Skin: Tummy time & skin to skin. Two things which can be important for your childs development.
  • 💊 Meds: Medication timers. For when youve given a dose and need to know when to give the next one.
  • 🏥 Hospital: Appointment/visit notes. A way to track your time in hospital should you ever have to.
  • 📊 Stats: Trends & export. If you need the numbers for a specialist or for your own planning.
  • 🛒 Deals: Best value nappies/formula. This is where I provide price per unit so that you can get the beat value.
  • ❄️ Stash: Manual freezer stash. If you store your milk in bags or bottles it can be good to know what you have in the freezer!
  • 📅 Appts: Upcoming appointments. This will help with appointment via letter or phone. At times we can be coordinating multiple locations and types of appointments.
  • 🌱 Milestones: Free text journal for thise personal key mile stones.
  • 💰 Savings: Money saving resources. These are what I have found has helped me at various stages. Sometimes as parents we juat need to go minimalist for a while to save some money or we end up in a situation where we need access to cheap food or items due to our circumstances.
  • 📋 Private Notes: Local only notes. These will not Sync to the cloud. Use this how you need to but be aware this information will not be saved.
  • 📍 Emergency: Nearby services. Good for when its 3am and you need a Pharmacy or you are on holiday and need an emergency GP.
  • 👤 Account: Family invite code, email used and other details you may need.
